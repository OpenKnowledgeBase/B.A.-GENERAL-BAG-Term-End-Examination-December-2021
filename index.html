Question 2: Make sentences using the following words

Words: Adapt, Aptitude, Incentive, Excel, Productive

Answers:

  • (a) Adapt: She quickly adapted to the new work environment.
  • (b) Aptitude: He has a natural aptitude for mathematics.
  • (c) Incentive: The company offered a bonus as an incentive for employees to meet their targets.
  • (d) Excel: She excels in her role as a team leader.
  • (e) Productive: The team had a productive meeting and achieved all their goals.

Question 5: Write short notes (around 150 words each)

The qualities of a good customer service executive

A good customer service executive should possess excellent communication skills, empathy, and patience. They must be able to listen actively to customers' concerns and provide clear, concise solutions. Problem-solving skills are essential to address issues effectively. Additionally, they should be knowledgeable about the company's products or services to provide accurate information. A positive attitude and the ability to remain calm under pressure are also crucial. Good customer service executives are proactive, ensuring that customers feel valued and satisfied with the service provided.

Organising a portfolio

Organising a portfolio involves systematically arranging and presenting your work, achievements, and skills in a structured manner. A well-organised portfolio showcases your professional journey and highlights your strengths. Start by categorizing your work into sections such as education, professional experience, skills, and projects. Use clear headings and subheadings to make it easy to navigate. Include relevant documents, certificates, and samples of your work. Ensure that your portfolio is visually appealing and free of errors. Regularly update it to reflect your latest accomplishments. A well-maintained portfolio not only helps in job applications but also serves as a personal record of your growth and achievements.
